### How to get pizza
- sit up straight
- turn to the left
- stand up
- turn to the right
- walk to the table
- grab a plate
- choose what kind of pizza you would like
- if the box is closed, open the right box
- choose which slice you would like
- remove the slice from the neighboring slices
- lift the slice
- place on the plate
- walk back to desk
- sit down
